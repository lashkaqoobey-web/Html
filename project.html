Current Projects

Here are some of the projects we are currently working on:

  • Community Coffee Shop: A new coffee shop designed to serve as a community hub for local coffee lovers.
  • Sustainable Sourcing Initiative: An effort to source our coffee beans from farms that prioritize environmental sustainability.
  • Coffee Education Program: A program aimed at teaching the public about the art and science of coffee brewing.
