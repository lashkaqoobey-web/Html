What We Offer

At CAAS Coffee, we provide a range of services to enhance your coffee experience:

Our Menu

  • Espresso: A strong and bold coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.
  • Cappuccino: A delicious blend of espresso, steamed milk, and frothy milk foam.
  • Latte: A creamy coffee made with espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of foam.
  • Mocha: A delightful combination of espresso, chocolate syrup, steamed milk, and whipped cream.
